Ideas

This page turns the catalogue into candidate secondary-analysis ideas. It is built around two core features: a Risk/Protective Factor Gap Matrix and a Research Question Generator. Together they identify datasets that combine gambling measures with risk or protective factors, highlight under-studied domains, and suggest cross-dataset research questions that may be worth developing.
